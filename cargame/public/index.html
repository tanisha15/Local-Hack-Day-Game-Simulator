About Game

Ace The Race
during this weekend!

This is a car game in which you have to dodge the coming cars.It displays 'YOU ARE CRASHED!' if you bump into another car or move out of the white boundary.Your score and the no of cars you have passed would be displayed in the left top corner of the window.The score will increase by 10 for each car you pass.Also there will be a level up for 10 cars passed.You can also speed up the acceleration of the obstacle cars or reduce the speed by applying brake.
